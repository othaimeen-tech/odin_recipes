Fish

It's an effortless 15 minute recipe one pan recipe where fish is baked with a creamy lemon sauce. It's the sort of recipe you can make no matter

Ingredients

  • Garlic powder or minced fresh garlic
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Soy sauce or Worcestershire sauce (for seasoning)
  • Capers or olives (for Mediterranean-style dishes)
  • Rice, quinoa, or couscous
  • Mashed potatoes or roasted potatoes
  • Salad greens or steamed vegetables

Steps

  1. Select a fresh fish fillet (such as salmon, cod, tilapia, trout, etc.) or a whole fish, depending on your preference.
  2. Pat the fish dry with paper towels to remove excess moisture, which helps achieve a crispy sear.
  3. Heat a skillet or frying pan over medium-high heat. Add a small amount of cooking oil with a high smoke point (such as olive oil, vegetable oil, or clarified butter).
  4. Cook without moving for 3-4 minutes, depending on the thickness of the fillet. Adjust cooking time accordingly (about 4-5 minutes per side per inch of thickness for most fillets).
  5. Add Additional Ingredients (Optional)
  6. Remove the fish from the pan and let it rest for a minute on a plate lined with paper towels to absorb any excess oil.
  7. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the fish to enhance its flavor.
  8. Serve the fish immediately, garnished with fresh herbs or alongside your favorite sides such as steamed vegetables, rice, or a salad.
