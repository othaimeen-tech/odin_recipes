lasagna

This easy Lasagna Recipe is meaty, cheesy, and flavorful. Perfect for a weeknight dinner or feeding a crowd, readers agree it's the best!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Egg (to bind the filling)
  • Sliced mushrooms
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce (Ragu)
  2. Drain excess fat if necessary.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes, tomato paste (if using), Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  4. Prepare the Cheese Mixture (Ricotta Filling)
  5. Cook the Lasagna Sheets (if using traditional)
  6. Assemble the Lasagna
  7. Bake the Lasagna
  8. Rest and Serve
