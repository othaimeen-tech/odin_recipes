Burger

A hamburger, also called a burger, is a dish consisting of fillings—usually a patty of ground meat, typically beef—placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoe
  • Cheese
  • Salad
  • Cucumber
  • Onion
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Ground Beef: Select high-quality ground beef with a good fat content (around 80% lean, 20% fat is often recommended for juiciness).
  2. Buns: Choose fresh burger buns. Brioche buns are popular for their slightly sweet flavor and soft texture.
  3. Cheese: Optional, but commonly used. Cheddar, American, or Swiss cheese are popular choices.
  4. Vegetables: Lettuce, tomato slices, onions (raw or caramelized), and pickles are common toppings.
  5. Condiments: Ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, barbecue sauce, or other preferred sauces.
  6. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes per side for medium doneness (adjust cooking time based on your preference for doneness).
  7. Serve immediately while the burger is warm and the cheese is melted.
